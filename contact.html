Frequently Asked Questions

What types of testing do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of chemical and material testing services including composition analysis, purity testing, material characterization, stability testing, and more. Our full service menu is available upon request.

How long does testing typically take?

Standard turnaround time is 5-7 business days. Rush services are available for an additional fee with results in as little as 48 hours for certain tests. We'll always provide an estimated completion date when we receive your samples.

Do you accept international clients?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. We can assist with shipping logistics and customs documentation for international sample submissions.

What are your pricing structures?

Pricing varies based on the specific tests required, sample quantity, and turnaround time. We offer volume discounts for recurring clients and can create customized testing packages to meet your budget and requirements.
