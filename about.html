Who We Are

LabPro Chem is a premier independent laboratory specializing in chemical analysis, material testing, and quality control services. Founded in 2010, we have grown to become a trusted partner for industries ranging from pharmaceuticals to advanced materials.

Our team of PhD chemists, material scientists, and laboratory technicians bring together decades of combined experience to deliver accurate, reliable, and timely results.

About Us – Labpro Chem

Labpro Chem is a trusted name in the chemical industry, dedicated to supplying high-quality laboratory and industrial chemicals to clients across India and beyond. Headquartered in Mumbai, we specialize in offering a wide range of fine chemicals, reagents, and raw materials used in research, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and various industrial applications.

With a strong focus on quality, reliability, and timely delivery, Labpro Chem has earned a reputation for excellence. We source our products from certified manufacturers and ensure they meet rigorous quality standards, backed by Certificates of Analysis (COA), Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS), and relevant documentation.

What Sets Us Apart:

  • Wide Product Range: From basic lab reagents to rare specialty chemicals
  • Reliable Service: Fast response, competitive pricing, and customer-first approach
  • Quality Assurance: Every batch tested and documented for purity and compliance
  • Pan-India Reach: Efficient logistics for timely delivery anywhere in India

At Labpro Chem, we don’t just deliver chemicals — we deliver confidence. Whether you are a research institute, industrial manufacturer, or pharmaceutical lab, we are committed to supporting your goals with dependable products and responsive service.
